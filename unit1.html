Introduction to Morgan Wallen

Morgan Wallen is a country music singer and songwriter that was born on May 13, 1993. He was born in Tennessee, and competed in the 6th season of The Voice. After being eliminated in the playoffs, he signed with Panacea Records, where he then released his deput EP Stand Alone in 2015.

Then, in 2016 he signed to Big Loud where he released a debut album called If I Know Me in 2018.
This album reached number 1 on the Billboard Top Country Albums chart.

2020 - Present

On May 27, 2020 He released "More Than My Hometown" to country radio as the lead single to his upcoming second studio album.

More Than My Hometown

Then, On August 14, Wallen released the song "7 Summers" and it broke the record for the most first day streams for a country song on Apple Music. That song earned him his first top 10 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart, debuting and peaking at number six. The song was also chose by the 'Time' as one of the best songs of 2020.
